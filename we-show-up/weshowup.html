We Show Up resource page

The We Show Up campaign is rooted in a deep understanding and appreciation of the essential human qualities that the people of HCA Healthcare possess. Compassion. Courage. Kindness. Perseverance. Caring. It honors the people behind the HCA Healthcare promise to show up – for our patients, for our communities and for each other. We will not be co-branding the campaign unless you are in a market that is placing paid advertising directed by the Nashville team.

What audience are we targeting?

Colleagues, nurses, physicians and other internal stakeholders. For example, ‘We Show Up’ assets can be used for internal events, town halls or within break rooms.

Who can access these assets?

Division Marketing VPs and Communications AVPs. We ask that you help us ensure that marketing and communications team are the owners and lead use and distribution of these assets while we work to build out our strategy for engaging other groups.
