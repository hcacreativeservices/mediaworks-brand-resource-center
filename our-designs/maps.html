Maps

National

> Go to National Maps

Our national map offers an impressive view of our national and global footprint. Its modern, clean design style features HCA Orange dots to clearly show current areas of business focus and future opportunity.

Division

> Go to Division Maps

In this division map, each division is indicated using a spoke and wheel icon. Each spoke represents a facility. In addition, remember that:

  • Colors are utilized from the primary color palette
  • The center of the wheel should include the number of facilities within a division
  • Each spoke should be a 0.5 px. stroke and lead from the wheel’s center to the facility location
  • You’ll need to spell out the division name and place it next to the wheel icon
