FAQs

What is MediaWorks used for?

MediaWorks is HCA's online tool for sharing, customizing, and managing digital and print-ready assets. Access images, content, video, print collateral and other media assets for a variety of service lines, initiatives and campaigns. From this enterprise repository, you can distribute collateral by downloading, sending to print, posting to social and sharing.

Who manages the Mediaworks site and its content?

The Creative Services team manages the MediaWorks platform and evaluates assets for brand alignment, quality and consistency. Thanks to the many contributors from across the enterprise, MediaWorks is a robust centralized repository of shared images, content, video, print collateral, and other media assets.

How do I update an existing asset or add a new one to MediaWorks?

Please submit a request through our intake form to add or update an asset on MediaWorks. Please note that not all MediaWorks assets are owned by Creative Services and some requests may need to be approved by a business owner.

What's the best way to find something in MediaWorks?

Try one of these options from the top navigation bar: the search field or Category menu.

Search. If you can’t find what you are looking for on MediaWorks, try using the search bar in the top navigation bar. Use the toggles to the right of the search bar to choose between searching the entire site or within your current category.

Advanced Search.Use advanced search to narrow your search results. This is helpful if you have an idea of date range, division, service line, etc. that you would like to search. To access advanced search options, click the arrow in the search field and click “Advanced Search.”

Category Menu You can explore assets and images by topic using the Category drop-down menu

Or, click the links in the Categories listed on the MediaWorks home page to explore available assets. If you are unable to find something, contact MediaWorks Support.

Is any training available for MediaWorks?

Click here to download the MediaWorks User Manual. A video version of this manual, along with a variety of guides and tutorials can be found on the MediaWorks Guides & Tutorials page.

MediaWorks Admins can find training documents and other resources on our MediaWorks Admin Support Page.

Please contact MediaWorks Support about any additional training.
