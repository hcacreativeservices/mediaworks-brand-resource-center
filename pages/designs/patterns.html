Our patterns

As part of our brand look and feel, approved patterns have been baked into branded assets to provide an extra visual touch. Each of our three patterns are available in multiple color combinations.

Patterns contain overlapping shapes that vary in scale and should maintain a monochromatic look and feel. Patterns are used to add texture to a designed item and may be used for most brand applications such as posters, one-pagers, desktop backgrounds, trade show booths, email headers or banners.

Source files for the patterns are contained in the designer resource section of the Brand Resource Center. In order to gain access to this section on the Brand Resource Center, you will need special MediaWorks access. Your HCA Healthcare manager should request Division Admin access for MediaWorks through eSAF.

Note: WCAG AA accessibility standards apply to normal and large text when placed on top of our patterns.

Please follow the below guidelines when modifying patterns:

Pieces can be moved around to fit with type or fill page, but keep overall proportion and spacing consistent with the original.

When working with type, keep color contrast rules in mind and place type only over areas that allow for sufficient contrast and are not busy.

Patterns should be used sparingly when working with imagery.

Patterns may be scaled up or down to fit the piece as long as they maintain proportions of the original.
