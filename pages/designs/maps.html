Our maps

A variety of maps are available to download. These maps are routinely updated and date stamped for accuracy. Examples of the maps are included below. If you need a custom map, please submit a request through Ask Brand.

Enterprise hospital map

This map offers a view of our national and global footprint. Its modern, clean design style features HCA Healthcare Navy dots to clearly show current hospital locations.

> Download enterprise hospital map

Enterprise divisions map

This map indicates each division using a singular number with radiating lines. Each line leads to a hospital.

> Download enterprise divisions map

Market map

Market maps provide a more focused view of the national map to give context to location and reveal specifics about a local market.

> Browse market maps

Facility map

These maps give context to locations using color and specific design elements. For example, light blue is used to symbolize bodies of water, green is used to symbolize parks, greenbelts and other natural open spaces, and a facility is indicated using a dot with the name placed nearby.

> Browse facility maps

Access point map

These maps showcase the locations a patient could receive treatment based on a specific region and service line.

> Browse access point maps
