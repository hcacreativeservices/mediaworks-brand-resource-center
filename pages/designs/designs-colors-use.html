How to use our colors

Use of color plays a significant role in our communications. Consistent use of color ensures that all of our communications identify quickly and easily as HCA Healthcare and build trust with our intended audiences.

Color balance

Varying combinations of primary and supporting colors may be necessary based on the individual application, but keep the following in mind:

  • Colors from the primary palette should always be dominant (at least 80%) in comparison to supporting colors (20% or less).
  • It is not necessary to include supporting colors. In the absence of supporting color, all three core colors or a fully HCA Healthcare Orange/white or HCA Healthcare Navy/white palette may be used.
  • White or white space should be as generous as possible.
  • Supporting colors may only be used when at least two colors from the primary color palette are present.
  • Any color usage exceptions must be approved by the Brand Team.
