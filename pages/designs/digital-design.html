Our digital design system

Our digital design system was created to tackle our organization’s most difficult challenges: efficiency and scalability. It is a guide to help designers, developers, other colleagues and vendor partners build products and applications that can scale to any
HCA Healthcare-owned brand and promote our overall brand promise. The system complies to ADA WCAG 2.0 AA Accessibility Guidelines. Our belief is that through this system, we allow colleagues to create from a core set of digital components and ideals, while also scaling to a product’s needs for responsive web, mobile apps, wearables, TVs, sound and other emerging technologies.

Examples of responsive web and iOS templates

Guiding principles

Simple

We take best practices in User Interface design and centralize them in a shared location. Components are informed by testing and research and developed in a way that can be easily consumed.

Consistent

We create a streamlined experience for users as they traverse the HCA Healthcare product suite. Developing this level of consistency builds trust and confidence for our patients and colleagues.

Efficient

We allow designers and developers on any product to pull from a well-defined source and hit the ground running. Reinventing the wheel or arguing over button colors are a thing of the past.

Scalable

Components can be altered to inherit any brand style for quick visualization. Components are designed to be scaled across multiple products, platforms and devices.

For detailed information about using our digital design system elements, navigation, content and language, please refer to the digital design guidelines.
