Accent Color Palette

Accent colors allow for flexibility in very specific cases where multiple colors are needed to express information clearly. Currently, they are approved for use only in maps, charts and graphs. Accent colors should be used as sparingly as possible and never used without the presence of at least one primary color. Suggested hierarchy is shown in order.
