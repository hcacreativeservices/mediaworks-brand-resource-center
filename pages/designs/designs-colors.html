Our colors

Color is an essential part of any brand and the HCA Healthcare brand is no different. We have a range of colors for our brand design system that can be used online or in print to create different expressions of our brand.

Primary color palette

The primary brand colors are HCA Healthcare Orange, HCA Healthcare Navy and white. Navy represents our legacy, while orange represents our energy. These colors are used in our logo and in every level of our brand design system (functional, flexible and free).

Secondary color palette

Secondary colors allow for adaptability in specific cases where multiple colors are needed to express information clearly or in a more creative way.

The secondary color palette is comprised of colors meant to complement the primary color palette. Crimson, Cherry Pie and Tangerine complement HCA Healthcare Orange. Royal Indigo, Denim and Fern complement HCA Healthcare Navy. These colors may be used with the flexible and free design styles.

Tertiary color palette

Tertiary colors allow for more creative expression in communications that benefit from added elasticity, as dictated by our design system.

Colors in the tertiary palette are primarily only used with the free design style. They may be used as accent colors in the flexible design style on a limited basis and as long as a primary or secondary color is the dominant color(s) in a communication.

Note: In addition to the HCA Healthcare brand colors, digital communications make use of additional colors to help communicate with users. Please see our Digital Design Guidelines section for more information.

Technical note: Each color has been optimized for print (Pantone® or 4-color process) or on-screen (RGB) reproduction. When specifying color for actual production, refer to these specifications. For 4-color process printing, use the specified CMYK color values. Apply the RGB or HTML color models for screen and web.

These color values are reflective of most print and digital projects.
