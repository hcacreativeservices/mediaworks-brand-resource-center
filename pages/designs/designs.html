Our brand typography

Primary font

Mark for HCA is the font for all headline copy and body copy. Its bold yet simple appearance catches the eye and mirrors our own bold desire to simplify the complexities of healthcare. When space permits, its bold version (non-italic) should be used in a large font size that calls for attention.

In the event you're unable to access this font, the system fallback is Arial.

Secondary font

Georgia Pro is the font often used for subhead copy and other isolated cases. The elegant serif font is a complement to the geometric style of Mark for HCA.

In the event you’re unable to access this font, the system fallback is Georgia.
