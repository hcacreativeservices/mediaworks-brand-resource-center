Our Brand Fonts

Mark for HCA

Mark for HCA is the font for all headline copy and body copy. Its bold yet simple appearance catches the eye and mirrors our own bold desire to simplify the complexities of healthcare. When space permits, its bold version (non-italic) should be used in a large font size that calls for attention.

Our brand fonts have been installed on all HCA Healthcare computers (contact your IT help desk if you are having trouble accessing these fonts). In the event you're unable to access this font, the system fallback is Arial.

Georgia Pro

Georgia Pro is the font for all subhead copy and other isolated cases. The elegant serif font is a complement to the geometric style of Mark for HCA. System and Google Web fonts are available.

Our brand fonts have been installed on all HCA Healthcare computers (contact your IT help desk if you are having trouble accessing these fonts). In the event you're unable to access this font, the system fallback is Georgia.
