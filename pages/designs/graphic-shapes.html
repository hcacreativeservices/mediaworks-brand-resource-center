Graphic shapes

Weâ€™ve developed a set of five graphic shapes for quick and easy visual recognition of our brand. These shapes help us create a consistent look and feel.

When using the shapes:

  • They may be scaled up or down for effect. When scaling, keep shape proportional to the overall size of the piece. Do not create mini or micro shapes.
  • Shapes may be rotated in 90-degree increments. The only exception is the arch, which may be rotated at any angle.
  • Do not use more than two shapes or types of shape on a page.
  • All shapes may be used as a mask for an image.

Source files for the graphic shapes can be found in the designer resource section.
