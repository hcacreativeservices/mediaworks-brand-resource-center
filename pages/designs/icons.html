Our decorative icons

Elevate your HCA Healthcare branded assets by using our decorative icons.

Decorative icons are intended to enhance the clarity of written communications, not to take their place. When using an icon alone, please be sure its meaning will be clear and quickly apparent to the intended audience. Icons may be edited with color to enhance or align with the unique needs of a particular piece. Do not modify the shape or lines of our icons or combine with icons from outside sets.
