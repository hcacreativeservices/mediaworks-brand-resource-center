Our Past. Our Future. Our Logo.

> Download the HCA Healthcare logo

Building a legacy worth looking back on takes a lot of looking forward. For 50 years we've focused on providing exceptional care today, while innovating the care of the future.

Our logo respects our rich history and illustrates our long-held vision of building a healthier tomorrow. It’s an iconic representation of our core care commitments and our duty to serve our patients.

Making Our Mark

The Diamond Plus mark is an important element of the HCA Healthcare logo, and it has unique significance.

Diamonds represent a life-long commitment and are most valuable when clear—much like
HCA Healthcare has clarity in its commitment to provide patients the care they deserve. In fact, the diamond in the center represents the patients we serve.

The HCA Healthcare logo was a collaborative effort between our internal creative team and a vendor partner over the course of about a year and a half. The video below is a quick peek into the iterative process-from discovery to completion-of bringing the spirit of our organization to life with a single visual mark.
