Making Our Mark

The Diamond Plus mark is an important element of the HCA Healthcare logo, and it has unique significance.

Diamonds represent a life-long commitment and are most valuable when clear—much like
HCA Healthcare has clarity in its commitment to provide patients the care they deserve. In fact, the diamond in the center represents the patients we serve.
