Our Past. Our Future. Our Logo.

Building a legacy worth looking back on takes a lot of looking forward. For 50 years, we've focused on providing exceptional care today, while innovating the care of the future.

Our new logo respects our rich history and illustrates our long-held vision of building a healthier tomorrow. It’s an iconic representation of our core care commitments and our duty to serve our patients.

Making Our Mark

The Diamond Plus mark is a new element of the HCA Healthcare logo. And it has unique significance.

Diamonds represent a life-long commitment and are most valuable when clear—much like HCA Healthcare has clarity in its commitment to provide patients the care they deserve. In fact, the diamond in the center represents the patients we serve.
