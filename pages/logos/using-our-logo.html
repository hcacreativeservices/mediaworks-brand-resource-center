How to use our logo

Clear space

The HCA Healthcare logo must always be surrounded by clear space that contains neither text nor graphics. The clear space surrounding the logo should be proportionate to the size of the Diamond Plus mark within the logo.

Logo clear space

Minimum size

The logo should always be at least 0.5 inches wide in print or 70 pixels wide when used digitally. The registration mark remains part of the logo lock-up when used at minimum size. However, for instances such as screen printing, embroidery or digital display, remove the registration mark if its legibility will be compromised at this small size.

Preferred color application

When placed on white, the HCA Healthcare logo is in HCA Healthcare Navy and the Diamond Plus is in HCA Healthcare Orange.

When placed on HCA Healthcare Navy, the HCA Healthcare logo is white and the Diamond Plus is in HCA Healthcare Orange.

1-Color usage

Though not preferred, 1-color black and 1-color white logos are available and are used when the logo is placed on a background that does not allow for enough contrast for full-color logo usage. For instance, when placed on HCA Healthcare Orange, the entire logo should be white, as shown. A 1-color logo may be placed on any approved HCA Healthcare brand color as long as there is enough contrast and it passes accessibility standards.

Whenever possible, follow the preferred color application. When color printing is not available or suitable due to cost or reproduction restrictions, use the 1-color black or 1-color white logo.

Please see the Color section for more information on color usage and contrast and accessibility standards.

Placement

Versions of the HCA Healthcare logo are available in both a stacked form, meaning HCA and the Diamond Plus rest atop the word Healthcare, and a horizontal form. The preferred logo usage is in its stacked form.

Registration mark

The registration mark is used in all applications of the logo, except when it is smaller than 0.5 inches wide in print or 70 pixels wide when used digitally*, or the medium makes the registration mark unreadable. It should always appear in this position and at this size relative to the logo. For high-cost, high-longevity uses (e.g., building signage), contact the Brand Team for guidance.

*These specifications are minimum size requirements. You must have Brand Team approval to use at a smaller size.
