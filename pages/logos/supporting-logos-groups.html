Groups

Definition

Large business entities within HCA Healthcare are considered groups. There are two types of groups: groups for operations and groups for large business areas.

The current groups at HCA Healthcare are: American Group, National Group, Operations and Service Line Group, Physician Services Group, Clinical Services Group, Human Resources Group and Information Technology Group.

Groups may be further divided into sub-departments.
