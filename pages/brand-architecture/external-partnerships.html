External partnerships

When can an external partner logo be co-branded?

There are two situations when HCA Healthcare would co-brand with an external partner. The first is when a formal partnership has been formed with the external entity and the external entity has control of the co-branding hierarchy (e.g., co-branding with Apple). In that scenario, we defer to their guidelines.

The second is a formal partnership with an external entity and HCA Healthcare has control of the co-branding hierarchy and thus is the priority brand. HCA Healthcare does not co-brand with vendors—therefore our logo should never appear with a vendor’s logo.
