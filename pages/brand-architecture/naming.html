Naming

Brand naming strategy

Our brand strategy is to drive growth by strengthening the equity of the HCA Healthcare enterprise brand over time. An important part of this strategy is the naming process used to create the name of new programs, products, services, etc. All names should support this strategy and help clearly communicate the product or service offering as descriptively as possible in order to strengthen the HCA Healthcare brand or associated market brand.

Always attempt to use an effective descriptive name whenever possible. Suggestive names should only be used when descriptive names are not feasible. Acronyms should not be used.

For help around naming, please reach out to Ask Brand. Refer to the Brand Book for additional information.
