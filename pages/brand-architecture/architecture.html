Architecture

Definition

Brand architecture defines how brands, programs, products, awards, etc. within our organization’s portfolio are organized, related to and differentiated from each other. Successful brand architecture provides context and offers a road map for future brand decisions around naming, logo development and co-branding.

Purpose

  • To increase value and reputation of the
    HCA Healthcare brand through brand management (i.e., not everyone gets a logo)
  • To avoid confusion through increased clarity of
    HCA Healthcare’s offerings and the value they bring to colleagues and other key stakeholders
  • To increase colleague awareness of the enterprise,
    HCA Healthcare, throughout the organization
