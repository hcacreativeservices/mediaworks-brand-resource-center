Brand outcomes

Enterprise logo

Our enterprise logo represents the entire HCA Healthcare community. Comprised of the Diamond Plus mark and our name in brand-specific font and colors, the HCA Healthcare logo represents who we are and the value we deliver.

Enterprise logo lock-up

Certain brands, such as the groups and divisions, are part of the
HCA Healthcare enterprise so their brand reflects the
HCA Healthcare logo, colors and fonts with a simple lock-up to indicate this close tie.

Enterprise sub-brand

Enterprise sub-brands align with the enterprise brand while also having semi-autonomous functions. Due to this semi-autonomous position, the enterprise sub-brands reflect the HCA Healthcare logo, font and colors, while also incorporating unique design elements at the Brand Teamâ€™s discretion. An example of an enterprise sub-brand is the Leadership Institute.

Core brand

Core brands align closely with
HCA Healthcare functions and mission. They are a core part of
HCA Healthcare and as such, core brands use the
HCA Healthcare logo while distinguishing themselves with their name in HCA Healthcare fonts and colors. These brand names can be descriptive or suggestive.

In the family brand

An in the family brand spans the HCA Healthcare organization but is not connected to the
HCA Healthcare enterprise in the mind of consumers. Instead, consumers associate these brands with their local access point. Therefore, these brands do not use the HCA Healthcare logo. They have a unique logo and incorporate the
HCA Healthcare fonts, colors and some design elements. An example of an in the family brand is MyHealthONE.

Independent brand

Independent brands have no ties to the HCA Healthcare brand elements as a means to distance them from the enterprise brand. These brands sell products or services to
HCA Healthcare competitors and thus should visually stand apart from the enterprise. Examples of independent brands are Parallon and Mobile Heartbeat.
