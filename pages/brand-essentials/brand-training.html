Brand training

Online brand training has been created to help colleagues familiarize themselves with the latest version of the HCA Healthcare Brand Book. Training topics include how to apply our tone of voice, lexicon and design system and tell our brand story, among others. Full-time colleagues may be assigned brand training in HealthStream, but it is available here for everyone. New colleagues, contractors and vendors working on behalf of the brand are all encouraged to take this training.

Looking forward, we hope to bring back in-person training in 2021.
