Talking to payers about HCA Healthcare

HCA Healthcare is a collaborative healthcare network, driven by physicians, nurses and colleagues helping each other champion the practice of medicine to give people a healthier tomorrow. As the largest private provider of healthcare in the United States, our scale enables us to deliver great outcomes for our patients, provide superior nursing care and be a preferred place for physicians to practice medicine. The strength and scale of our nationally integrated network, combined with our unique operational discipline, delivers a more effective and more efficient provider system in the communities we serve, which keeps costs down and focuses investment where it really matters: on our patients. Along with our growing network of hospitals, we also serve patients through an expansive outpatient network, which includes emergency rooms, urgent care clinics, ambulatory surgery centers, physician practices and outpatient imaging centers. This network supports health plans’ need to keep members in-network while offering them a wide array of quality care choices. As health plan providers, you face increasing pressure to connect members to quality medical services and convenient care. HCA Healthcare is uniquely prepared to ease this pressure through our vast network and innovative approaches.
