Talking to payers about HCA Healthcare

Last updated 12/19/2019

HCA Healthcare’s scale and services are changing more lives in more ways:

  • We are a collaborative healthcare network, driven by physicians, nurses and colleagues.
  • We are the largest private provider of healthcare in the United States.
  • We are able to deliver great outcomes for our patients, provide superior nursing care and be a preferred place for physicians to practice medicine.
  • We are a nationally integrated network with a unique operational discipline that delivers a more effective, more efficient provider system in the communities we serve.
  • We keep costs down and focus investment where it really matters: on our patients.
  • We operate hospitals, emergency rooms, urgent care clinics, ambulatory surgery centers, physician practices and outpatient imaging centers, all to serve an expansive community of patients.
  • We support health plans’ need to keep members in-network while offering them a wide array of quality care choices.
  • We have a vast network and innovative approaches to providing quality services and convenient care.
