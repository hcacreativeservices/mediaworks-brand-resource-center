Calls-to-action

Creating the right call-to-action (CTA) is critical for getting the desired audience response. Certain communications must have a CTA so that people can engage, get informational and emotional benefits and stay in the experience for as long as possible.

When writing a CTA, identify where your communication/asset lives in the user experience and ensure it drives to the next step in the path. Be sure to use clear, actionable verbs and keep your CTA short.
