Employee value proposition tenets

An employee value proposition (EVP) expresses the core reasons people are proud and motivated to work for an organization. At HCA Healthcare, our EVP is made up of three tenets: raise the bar, unlock possibilities and care like family. These phrases explain how colleagues benefit from working with HCA Healthcare in a few words.

When using the EVP tenets in communications, do not overuse them or treat them as taglines. Instead, use them as guiding pillars whose meaning is infused into communications for our colleagues or job candidates.

You can use the tenets verbatim or paraphrase them to avoid overusing them. Here are some examples:

  • Through our Unit of Distinction program, we celebrate nursing units that are raising the bar for the nursing profession.
  • Every day, we seek to raise the bar higher, not just for ourselves, but for healthcare everywhere.
  • Each member of the care team plays a unique and vital role in raising the bar for best-in-class, patient-centered care.
  • Our dedication to caring like family extends beyond the walls of our facilities into the communities where we live and work.
  • In moments of darkness, HCA Healthcare’s beliefs in service to others, giving back to the community and caring like family stand out like a beacon of hope.
  • We’ve built a unique culture in which we do our best to care for each other and our patients like family.
  • Our scale enables us to unlock numerous possibilities for our colleagues to learn, grow and enjoy a rewarding career.
  • HCA Healthcare partners with other leading organizations to unlock more possibilities for improving health and well-being.
  • A few of our HCA Healthcare colleagues share about their HCA Healthcare experiences and how they work to unlock possibilities to create positive impact.

You may have noticed that the EVP tenets are not the main focus of any of these statements. These sentences focus on other areas of the organization, but the EVP tenets are included to subtly reinforce the value of working with HCA Healthcare.
