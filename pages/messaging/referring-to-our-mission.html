Our mission

As mentioned earlier, the HCA Healthcare brand is a direct reflection of our mission: Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life. This statement is the guiding compass of our organization. When referring to our mission, we ask that you use the following guidance:

Don’t use the word mission to refer to ideas that are clearly different than our mission statement. Some paraphrasing of our mission is acceptable as long as the main idea is intact.

“we all contribute to our mission of elevating the standards of care in our communities”

“we all contribute to our goal of elevating the standards of care in our communities”

“we all contribute to our mission: above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life”

“we all contribute to our mission of caring for and improving human life”

Refer to Above all else only in its original context in our mission.

“Above all else, we need to be pillars of health in our communities.”

“It’s important that we be seen as pillars of health in our communities.”

“Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.”
