Words to use

There are words that we prefer to use more frequently and those we prefer to use sparingly. In general, default to our preferred words. Itâ€™s important to consider the audience and context when determining which words will best serve the situation.

Use Instead of
Colleague Employee*
We/our hospitals/facilities/organization/enterprise Corporation/company
Purpose/goal/focus Mission*
Scale/a leading Size/biggest/largest
Tools/treatment/technology Clinical services

*Employee may be used when explicitly referring to individuals employed by HCA Healthcare, when the audience requires a more formal tone (e.g., business settings) or in communications when it must be used (e.g., Human Resources or legal communications)

**Mission should only be used when referring to our mission statement: Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.
