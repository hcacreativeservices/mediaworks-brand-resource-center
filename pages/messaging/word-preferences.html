Our Messaging

Word preferences

Our word choices and writing style convey a message about who we are, and we want to ensure that we speak with a unified, consistent voice. It’s why we’ve put together a lexicon and guidelines: the clearer and more consistent we can all be, the easier it will be for our audiences to know who we are and what we stand for.

Use Instead of
Colleague Employee
We/our hospitals/facilities/organization/enterprise Corporation/company
Purpose/goal/focus Mission*
Scale/a leading Size/biggest/largest
Tools/treatment/technology Clinical services**

*Only our mission statement should be referred to as our mission: Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.

**Use clinical services only when referring to the Clinical Services Group.

These messaging preferences should be considered based on your audience:

When addressing colleagues, patients or consumers, talk about When addressing investors or business leaders, it may be more appropriate to talk about
Community Market
Healthcare/the healthcare field Industry
Patient-centered/focused or person-centered/focused For-profit/investor-owned/tax-paying
Patients are our top priority Market share/operating margins
Patient results/outcomes Profits/profitability/quality metrics
