Word and phrase suggestions

Below are the words and phrases we strive to incorporate into our communications. While it’s not necessary to use all of them in all pieces, you can use them to help guide you as you write. The goal with the phrases is to be simple. It’s about finding the core of the idea and stripping our language down to its most critical essence by weeding out unnecessary elements.

Designated HCA Healthcare hashtags are also suggested below. Using hashtags via our social media accounts allows our audience to find our posts and helps to categorize content for them.

For more word and phrase suggestions, visit the lexicon section of the Brand Book.

***#HCACares is to be used when speaking to community service/philanthropic efforts.

**Raise the bar, unlock possibilities and care like family are our Employee Value Proposition (EVP) tenets. An EVP expresses the core reasons people are proud and motivated to work for an organization. They explain in a few words how colleagues benefit from working with HCA Healthcare. Do not overuse the EVP tenets or treat them as taglines. Instead, use them as guiding pillars whose meaning is infused into the communications you craft for our colleagues or job candidates.
