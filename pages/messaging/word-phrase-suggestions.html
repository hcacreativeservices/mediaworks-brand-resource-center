Phrases to use

Below are the words and phrases we strive to incorporate into our communications. While it’s not necessary to use all of them in all pieces, you can use them to help guide you as you write. The goal with these phrases is to keep it simple. It’s about finding the core of the idea and stripping the language down to its most critical essence by weeding out unnecessary elements.

Designated HCA Healthcare hashtags are also suggested below. Using hashtags via our social media accounts allows our audience to find our posts and helps categorize content for them.
