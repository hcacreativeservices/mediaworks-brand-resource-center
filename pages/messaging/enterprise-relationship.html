Talking about enterprise relationship

Last updated 12/19/2019

Please find below suggested supportive language for our divisions and hospitals to consider using when explaining the relationship between their division/hospital and the HCA Healthcare enterprise.

  • [Insert division/hospital] is a part of HCA Healthcare and together we improve more lives in more ways. As a part of HCA Healthcare, our colleagues are connected to something bigger, which means more resources, more solutions and more possibilities for everyone who walks through our doors. Every day, we seek to raise the bar higher, not just for ourselves, but for healthcare everywhere.
  • We’re proud to be a part of HCA Healthcare, driven by our collective mission: Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.
  • Insert division/hospital] is a part of HCA Healthcare, a healthcare network comprised of a more than 270,000 strong workforce. We are bound together by a single purpose: to create healthier tomorrows for every patient we serve – and for each other as well.
  • We are proud to be a part of HCA Healthcare, a collaborative healthcare network comprised of hospitals, urgent care facilities, physician offices and other care sites located in 21 states and the United Kingdom. Our colleagues’ skill and sense of purpose drive our commitment to delivering excellence through effective, evidence-based, innovative and patient-centered care.
  • [Insert division/hospital] is a part of HCA Healthcare, comprised of 184 hospitals and approximately 2,000 sites of care. Our scale enables us to unlock numerous opportunities for our colleagues to learn, grow and enjoy a rewarding career.
