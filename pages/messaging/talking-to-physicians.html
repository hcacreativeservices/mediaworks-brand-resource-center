Talking to physicians about HCA Healthcare

HCA Healthcare was founded by two physicians, a father and son, who believed that the best care and outcomes are the result of empowered physicians. Today, we continue to be a collaborative national network of locally managed healthcare facilities, driven by physicians helping each other champion the practice of medicine for a healthier world.

We’re not only here for physicians—we are here because of them, and we are dedicated to their professional development and leadership opportunities. HCA Healthcare knows that physicians are the experts at providing care for patients, so we are committed to giving each one a strong voice and offering them the clinical capabilities they need, including a premier learning medical environment, state-of-the-art facilities and confidence in great nursing care.

Ultimately, healthcare’s promise is fulfilled when each patient’s health is restored or preserved by virtue of the dedicated work of the physician. It is for the better care of each patient that we are constantly evolving, improving and impacting national standards of care. But no matter how we evolve, we will never lose sight of our mission: above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.
