Talking about our brand

Talking to HCA Healthcare colleagues about the brand initiative

The new HCA Healthcare brand is a statement of our collective purpose— what you and your colleagues do every day and why we exist as an organization: to give people a healthier tomorrow. This statement of purpose—to give people a healthier tomorrow—is new. But our purpose is more than a new phrase. It is anchored by a set of beliefs that drive the “how” and “why” we show up for work every day; what we collectively stand for; and, ultimately, how we want patients to feel when they set foot in any one of our facilities. No matter your role or your location at any of our 1,800 care facilities, this purpose connects all of us as an organization.

  • Getting the HCA Healthcare brand right is a business imperative. We must be more intentional in explaining who we are, what we stand for and the advantages of our scale–the branding initiative is a significant step forward.
  • The HCA Healthcare brand is more than a new logo; it will help all 270,000 of us uniformly convey our purpose and our story, and communicate with one voice.
  • Our mission is not changing; it is the foundation of our brand.
  • Our journey to the new brand began by listening to our colleagues. We heard that our scale, people and forward-thinking approach allow us to have a positive impact beyond any other organization in the industry.
  • A differentiated brand will help us engage our colleagues and recruit the most talented candidates–both of which are central to our business.
