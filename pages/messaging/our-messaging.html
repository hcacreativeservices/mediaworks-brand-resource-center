Talking about our brand

How we use these messages

How we talk about ourselves and our colleagues is just as important as our look and logo. The following messages are intended to help us all share the HCA Healthcare story in a unified and consistent way. The clearer and more consistent we can all be, the easier it will be for our audiences to know who we are and what we stand for. That being said, these messages are living and evolving, so please always refer back to the Brand Resource Center for the most up-to-date messaging.

Cut and paste the entire message, or use just the parts you need. If you are using a message that contains a number or data point, be sure to date stamp your end product either within the text or in a footnote. Consistently using and understanding these messages will help all of us bring the new HCA Healthcare brand to life.
