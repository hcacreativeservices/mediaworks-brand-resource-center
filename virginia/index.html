This fall, all of our Virginia hospitals, physician practices and other sites of care are uniting under a new brand as HCA Virginia Healthcare.

The new market brand aligns with our HCA Healthcare enterprise brand and other HCA Healthcare market brands to showcase the many ways you show up for our patients, colleagues, physicians and communities.

As a member of the HCA Virginia Healthcare family, you are connected to something bigger. This means more solutions and more possibilities for you during every step of your career. Wherever you work throughout the state, you are part of a network of colleagues who work together to deliver advanced, collaborative care.

While our look and feel are changing, we remain committed to providing the same excellent care that our patients expect. Our brand will celebrate the best of who we are and strengthen our culture through unity.

Our commitment to you

infographic

When you believe in something bigger than yourself, it shows. At HCA Virginia Healthcare, you are making patients your highest priority and setting the standard for quality care. Beyond names and logos, our brand comes to life in the ways you care for our patients and each other every day. Every interaction you have with a patient impacts their experience with our brand – from how they are greeted when they walk through the door, to the specialized care they receive.

Protecting and supporting our colleagues, nurses and physicians is one of our top priorities. Our 16,000 colleagues in Virginia have access to a breadth of opportunities to learn and grow at every stage of their career.

As part of HCA Healthcare, you have access to a variety of programs and benefits to support our colleagues and their families, including:

  • Supporting your physical, mental and emotional well-being through new and expanded programs, including Nurse Care and HCA Healthcare’s Wellbeing Program.
  • Assisting with your professional development, including tuition reimbursement, certification assistance and scholarships for eligible employee dependents.
  • Expanding access to industry-leading education and career development opportunities in the state, especially through our partnership with Galen College of Nursing.
  • Industry-leading graduate medical education (GME) programs that ensure graduates joining an HCA Healthcare residency or fellowship program have extensive training and support from a dynamic network of physicians.
  • 188 residents and fellows at two teaching hospitals in Virginia
