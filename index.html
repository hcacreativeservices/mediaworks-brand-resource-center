Strengthening our purpose through our new, unified brand.

The Brand Resource Center is your destination for all things brand. It’s where you’ll find everything you need to consistently maintain our new brand identity and connect our 250,000 colleagues.

Because when we know how to speak in a unified way about HCA Healthcare, we can better communicate our brand promise to improve more lives in more ways. Being a brand advocate will and help us raise the bar higher for patient experience, quality of care and positive outcomes.

As we evolve, this is where you’ll find essential updates and information: Everything from guidelines and logos to design elements, templates and tools. This site should answer most of your questions out the gate—but if not, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
