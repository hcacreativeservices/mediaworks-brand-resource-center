Strengthening our purpose through our new, unified brand.

The Brand Resource Center is your destination for all things brand. It’s where you’ll find everything you need to consistently maintain our new brand identity and connect our 265,000 colleagues.

Everything from guidelines and logos to design elements, templates and tools will be consistently updated and available here.
